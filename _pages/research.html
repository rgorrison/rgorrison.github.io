--- layout: archive title: "Research" permalink: /research/ author_profile: true --- I study the variability of the global climate system, and the sensitivity of the climate system to internal and external forcings. I study this by looking at a combination of paleoclimate evidence and Earth system models to study how dynamical processes interact across temporal and spatial scales.
