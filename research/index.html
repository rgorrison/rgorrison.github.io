I utilize diverse data sources and statistical methods to better understand variability and change within our natural climate system across scales of space and time. I am curious about how natural variability is influenced by external factors such as human activity and natural processes. I am particularly interested in how socioeconomic and environmental histories influence community resiliency and/or vulnerability to a changing climate.

Below you can find more information about a few distinct research projects from my work in graduate school.
